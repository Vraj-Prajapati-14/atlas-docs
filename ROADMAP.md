# 1. Purpose

This roadmap defines the long-term execution plan for Project Atlas.

It provides a structured sequence for transforming a business idea into an enterprise-grade Vertical SaaS platform.

The roadmap exists to ensure that no engineering work begins without sufficient business understanding and architectural planning.

---

# 2. Guiding Principles

Project Atlas follows five fundamental principles.

## 2.1 Business Before Code

Understand the business completely before implementing software.

---

## 2.2 Research Before Decisions

Every significant architectural or product decision must be backed by research.

---

## 2.3 Design Before Development

All major workflows, APIs, database models, and business rules must be documented before implementation.

---

## 2.4 Build Incrementally

Deliver value in small, validated increments while preserving long-term architectural quality.

---

## 2.5 Document Everything

Every important decision must be documented.

No undocumented feature should exist.

# 5. Definition of Done

A phase is considered complete only when:

* Deliverables are complete.
* Documentation is reviewed.
* Open questions are resolved.
* Risks are documented.
* Dependencies are satisfied.
* Approval has been granted.
